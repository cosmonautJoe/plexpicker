How do I find my token manually?
  1. Sign in to Plex in a browser at app.plex.tv.
  2. Open any movie, click the ⋮ menu, and choose Get Info.
  3. Click View XML at the bottom-left of the info window.
  4. In the URL that opens, copy everything after X-Plex-Token= and paste it above.
